About Me

I am currently a Freshman at the University of Boulder Colorado studying Creative Technology and Design with a minor in Computer Science. I choose this major not only because of how unique it was but because of how taylored students can make it to themselves. I plan on using the resources here to learn the fundementals of game development in hopes of one day securing a job as a game developer!

My experience with tutoring kids mathematics at Mathnasium and helping different customers thoughout my time at the Utah Olympic Park have helped me develop a strong communication skill and people management ability.

Outside of my studies and work, I love photographing people, landscapes, and architecture! Through years of practice I capture not only a moment in time but the emotions with it, allowing me to show others just what I want them to see and feel.
